Privacy Policy — Beast AI Marketing

Last updated: 2025-10-22

Beast AI Marketing (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates an AI marketing automation platform that connects to third-party services (such as TikTok, YouTube, Google, PayPal, and OpenAI) to provide automated content publishing and analytics tools.

Information We Collect

We collect information only when you explicitly grant access via OAuth, including basic profile information, email address, and access tokens necessary to perform actions on your behalf (posting, reading analytics).

How We Use Information

  • To publish content and manage scheduled posts.
  • To read analytics and generate reports.
  • To send transactional emails and notifications when authorized.

Token & Credential Handling

Access tokens and refresh tokens are stored encrypted and used solely to perform the actions you authorize. You may revoke access at any time through your provider account settings.

Privacy Policy – Data Access and Sharing

The Beast app may access your accounts and data from Google, TikTok, and other connected services to provide its full functionality. Specifically, the app may use:

  • Google Gmail: read, compose, and send emails on your behalf.
  • Google Sheets: view, edit, create, and delete spreadsheets.
  • YouTube Data: manage your YouTube account, including videos, playlists, comments, and analytics.
  • TikTok: post, manage, and analyze content on your TikTok account.

Data Sharing and Third-Party Access

All account data accessed by the Beast app is used solely to provide app functionality. Your information may be shared only with our trusted service providers to perform necessary operational tasks, such as sending emails, managing spreadsheets, or posting content. We do not sell, rent, or disclose your data to any other third parties. All data access is strictly limited to what is necessary for the Beast app to function.
